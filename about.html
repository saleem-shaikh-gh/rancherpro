About Us

Rancher Pro L.L.P. is a limited liability partnership firm incorporated on December 9, 2020. It is registered at the Registrar of Companies in Mumbai. Its total obligation for contribution is Rs. 1500,000.

We are a major exporter and importer of dates and dried fruits. We sell grocery and fast-moving consumer goods, as well as fruits and vegetables. We are growing our business in India. We have our own brand name, "LIMYA" which has been registered by our company and listed.

We want to provide the country with high-quality date and dry fruit products that will help India become healthier. We want to rid India of preservatives and serve organic products to the people.
